Accommodation in Bulgaria

Bulgaria offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit different travel styles and budgets. From luxury hotels in major cities to traditional guesthouses in rural villages, visitors can find many comfortable places to stay while exploring the country.

In large cities such as Sofia and Plovdiv, modern hotels provide convenient access to tourist attractions, restaurants, and cultural landmarks. Many of these hotels offer high quality services and facilities that cater to international visitors.

Along the Black Sea coast, large resort hotels are common and provide easy access to beaches, entertainment, and recreational activities. These resorts are especially popular during the summer tourist season.

For travelers seeking a more traditional experience, guesthouses and small family run accommodations are available throughout the countryside and mountain regions. These places often offer homemade meals and a welcoming atmosphere that allows visitors to experience Bulgarian hospitality firsthand.
