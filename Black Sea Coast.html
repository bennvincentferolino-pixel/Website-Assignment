Black Sea Coast

The Black Sea coast is one of Bulgaria’s most popular tourist regions. Stretching along the eastern side of the country, the coastline features sandy beaches, seaside towns, and vibrant summer resorts. During the warmer months, tourists from around the world visit the Bulgarian coast to enjoy the sun, sea, and lively atmosphere.

Sunny Beach is one of the largest and most famous resorts on the coast. It is known for its wide sandy beaches, hotels, restaurants, and nightlife. Many visitors come here to relax, swim, and enjoy entertainment throughout the summer season.

Golden Sands is another well known resort area located near the city of Varna. The resort is surrounded by natural parks and forests, which makes it a beautiful place for both relaxation and outdoor activities.

In addition to modern resorts, the coast is also home to historic towns such as Nessebar. This town is famous for its ancient churches, cobblestone streets, and charming seaside atmosphere. Because of its historical importance, Nessebar has been designated as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
